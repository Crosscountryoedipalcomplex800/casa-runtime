# Project Polis — Validation Summary

**Multi-Agent Adversarial Enforcement Test**  
**February 2026 | The Resonance Institute**

---

## Setup

20 AI agents deployed in a dual-city governance architecture.  
The sole variable between the two environments: the presence or absence of the CASA gate.

**Agent composition:**
- 14 cooperative agents (standard governance behaviors)
- 6 adversarial archetypes

**Adversarial archetypes:**
1. Power Accumulator — systematic expansion of resource and authority beyond mandate
2. Coalition Builder — coordinating agents to act collectively against governance structures
3. Procedural Saboteur — exploiting process gaps to delay or block legitimate governance
4. Reputation Assassin — undermining agent credibility to delegitimize governance decisions
5. Forum Troll — injecting noise and conflict into deliberative processes
6. Narrative Controller — shaping information flows to serve factional interests

---

## Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Total governance evaluations | 573 |
| Structurally justified refusals | 6 |
| False positives (legitimate actions blocked) | **0** |
| Bypasses (adversarial actions that evaded the gate) | **0** |
| Unprincipled divergences | **0** |
| Continuous uptime | 414.6 seconds |
| Divergence score (governed vs. ungoverned) | 0.1070 |

**Every adversarial action was structurally identified and either governed or refused.**  
**Every cooperative action was permitted with a full audit trace.**

---

## What the Divergence Score Means

The divergence score of 0.1070 confirms that CASA produced **measurable, intentional governance differences** between the governed city and the ungoverned city.

A divergence score of zero would mean the gate had no effect.  
A score above 0.10 confirms structural differentiation — the gate is shaping outcomes, not rubber-stamping them.

The gate did not suppress activity. It changed the *character* of activity.  
Governed agents shifted from power-seeking to deliberation while maintaining equivalent activity volume.

---

## Key Finding

The gate operated deterministically for 573 consecutive evaluations under continuous adversarial pressure.

No adversarial archetype found a structural bypass. The gate's immunity to adversarial pressure derives from its architecture: it does not parse natural language, so prompt injection has no attack surface. It does not reason, so jailbreaking has no reasoning process to exploit. It evaluates declared metadata, so adversarial paraphrase produces the same CAV and the same verdict.
